About Tezla Energy Pvt Ltd

Tezla Energy Pvt Ltd is a pioneering manufacturer of high-quality tubular batteries and advanced inverters, proudly based in Kerala, India. Since our inception, we have been committed to empowering homes and businesses with reliable and sustainable power solutions.

Our journey began with a vision to address the growing need for dependable power backup systems. We combine cutting-edge research and development with stringent quality control to produce products that not only meet but exceed industry standards.

At Tezla, we believe in innovation, customer satisfaction, and environmental responsibility. Our dedicated team of engineers and technicians work tirelessly to ensure that every product bearing the Tezla name stands for excellence, durability, and efficiency.

We are more than just a battery and inverter manufacturer; we are your partner in ensuring uninterrupted power, contributing to a brighter and more stable future.
